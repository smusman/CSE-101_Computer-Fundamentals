The Master's degree programme at University of Engineering and Technology shall extend over a period of at least four semesters. A total of 32 credit hours (including 26 credit hours of courses and 6 credit hours of research) are required to complete the Master's Degree Programme. The 26 credit hours of course work shall include a minimum of 18 credit hours from the area of specialization and 2 credit hours of the course "Research Methodology". This course will be registered as credit course.

M.Sc Programmes Offered:

  • Computer Systems Engineering
  • Computer Software Engineering
